The ShutterNG Story.

ShutterNG didn't start in a boardroom... it started on a rainy day in Lagos, inside a cramped studio with one broken tripod and a camera that overheated after 10 minutes.

Our founder was just a passionate photographer, hustling to shoot birthday gigs and pre-wedding shoots, renting overpriced gear from unreliable sources. One day, after showing up for a big client only to realize the rented lens was fogged inside, something snapped.

"There's got to be a better way for creators in Nigeria to get good gear."

So he did something wild.. he sold his PS4, took a loan from a friend, and bought a used Canon 80D. From there, he started lending it to other creatives for the price of a shawarma and transport. No forms. No stress. Just trust.

That camera became the first in a growing lineup.

Word spread. Creators started reaching out. Music video directors, TikTokers, vloggers, even churches filming online sermons. Everyone needed gear. Everyone needed someone they could trust.

That's when ShutterNG was born, not just as a camera store, but as a movement for the creative underground. A place where photographers, filmmakers, and storytellers could find reliable equipment without breaking the bank or breaking their spirit.

Today, we're still built on that same passion

Real gear. Real support. Real community.

From side hustlers to full-time pros, ShutterNG is here to make sure no one ever has to pause their vision because of a fogged lens again.
